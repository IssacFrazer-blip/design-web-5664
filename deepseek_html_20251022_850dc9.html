About Us

Our History

Founded in 2015 by former Sierra Leone international Mohamed Jalloh, Salone Football Connect emerged from a vision to create professional opportunities for talented footballers across Sierra Leone.

Starting with just five local players, we've grown to represent over 50 professional footballers competing in leagues across Africa, Europe, and Asia.

Our commitment to ethical representation and player development has made us the most trusted football agency in Sierra Leone.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Sierra Leonean footballers are consistently competing at the highest levels of world football, with proper representation and career guidance.

Our mission extends beyond transfers - we're building a sustainable football ecosystem in Sierra Leone through youth development programs and coach education initiatives.

We measure our success not just in transfer fees, but in the lifelong careers and positive impact our players make in their communities.
